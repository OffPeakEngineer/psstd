## Problem

pulsed is a monitoring surface people may leave open, but they may also need to quickly inspect it without a mouse. Small keyboard and focus improvements can make the dashboard feel more deliberate without adding major UI.

## Done when

- Theme and palette controls have clear focus states
- Node links are reachable and understandable by keyboard
- Any future sort/filter controls work without mouse-only interactions
- Accessibility improvements do not add a large client-side app framework
